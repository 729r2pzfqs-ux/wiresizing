AWG to mm² Formula

The cross-sectional area in mm² for any AWG gauge can be calculated:

Area (mm²) = (π/4) × [0.127 × 92(36-n)/39]²

Note that AWG and IEC metric sizes don't have exact 1:1 equivalents. A 12 AWG wire (3.31 mm²) is closest to the 4 mm² IEC standard size, but they are not identical.
