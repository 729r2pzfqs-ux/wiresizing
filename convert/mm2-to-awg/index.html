Note: AWG and IEC metric sizes are independent systems — there are no exact equivalents. The AWG values shown are the closest match by cross-sectional area. A 2.5 mm² metric wire (closest to 13 AWG) has a slightly different area than the exact AWG equivalent. Always verify the actual specifications when substituting between systems.
