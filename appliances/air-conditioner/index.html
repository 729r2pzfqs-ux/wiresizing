Central Air Conditioner — Wire Size & Circuit Requirements

Central AC units typically draw 15-25 amps depending on size (2-5 ton). A 3-ton unit typically draws about 18-20 amps.

Important Notes

AC units have high starting (LRA) currents that can be 3-5× running amps. The breaker and wire must handle the maximum circuit ampacity marked on the unit's nameplate. Check the MCA (Minimum Circuit Ampacity) and MOP (Maximum Overcurrent Protection) on the data plate.
